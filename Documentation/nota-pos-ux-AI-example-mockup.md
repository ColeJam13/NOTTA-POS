## Design System Summary

**Colors (8-bit Retro Aesthetic):**
- Primary: Purple/Magenta (`#B026FF`, `#8B00FF`)
- Success: Neon Green (`#00FF00`, `#39FF14`)
- Warning: Neon Orange (`#FF6600`, `#FF9500`)
- Danger: Red (`#FF0000`, `#FF3333`)
- Background: Dark (`#1A1A2E`, `#16213E`)
- Text: White/Off-white (`#FFFFFF`, `#E0E0E0`)

**Typography:**
- Headers: Bold, uppercase, retro gaming font
- Body: Clean, readable sans-serif
- Numbers: Monospace for financial data

**Icons:**
- Use emoji for quick visual recognition
- Supplement with simple geometric shapes
- Maintain consistent sizing (large touch targets)

**Spacing:**
- Generous padding for touch targets (minimum 44x44px)
- Clear visual separation between cards/sections
- Breathing room for dense information

**Interaction Patterns:**
- Tap to select/view details
- Swipe actions for quick operations (future)
- Long-press for additional options (future)
- Progress bars for time-sensitive actions (delay timer!)
